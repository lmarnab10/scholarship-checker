About Uluberia Government Polytechnic

Uluberia Government Polytechnic, established in the academic session 2021–22, is a technical institute under the Government of West Bengal. The institute offers AICTE-approved diploma courses in:

  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Computer Science and Technology
  • Chemical Engineering

Our aim is to produce skilled and knowledgeable diploma engineers who will contribute significantly to the industry and society.

About Scholarships

Students of our institute are eligible for the following major scholarship schemes:

1. SVMCM (Swami Vivekananda Merit-cum-Means Scholarship)

Offered by the Government of West Bengal for meritorious students based on academic performance. Students must score at least 60% in the previous examination to be eligible. Link: svmcm.wb.gov.in.

List of Documents Required for Submission at the College:

1) Duly filled Application Form

2)Marksheet of Madhyamik Examination or its equivalent

3)Marksheet of the most recent board examination

4)Admission Receipt

5)Income Certificate

6)Domicile Proof – any one of the following: Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/Ration Card/Certificate issued by the competent authority

7)Photocopy of Bank Passbook (first page showing name and account details)

8)Year Gap Certificate (if applicable).

2. OASIS (For SC/ST/OBC Students)

A state government scholarship dedicated to students from Scheduled Castes, Scheduled Tribes, and OBC communities. It provides financial support to reduce dropout rates in higher education.

3. Aikyashree (For Minority Students)

Managed by the West Bengal Minorities Development & Finance Corporation, this scholarship supports students from minority communities (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi) to pursue education without financial hardship.

4. NSP (National Scholarship Portal)

A central government scholarship platform hosting various schemes from different ministries and state governments. Students can apply online at scholarships.gov.in.
